01 Why Caravel

Talent is everywhere.
Momentum isn't.

Too many good ideas stay trapped in a notebook, waiting for the right person, skill, or push.

Caravel started with a simple belief: Tunisia has no shortage of bright minds. What we need are more places for them to collide—openly, generously, and with purpose.

So we're building that place. A community that helps you find your people, sharpen your thinking, and move from “what if?” to “what's next?”

Not an audience.
A crew.

Caravel is built with its community—not simply for it.
